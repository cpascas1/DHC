Itemized Requests

What's the difference of Copy of Receipt, Itemized Receipt, and Itemized Bill?

Copy of Receipt - contains the patient payment, method used and authorization that was originally sent to their email address when they made the payment.

Itemized Receipt - is a detailed payment receipt that contains Service Date, Procedure, Charges, Payment posting date, Payment from patient, Matching amount from the payment, Payment method and Receipt number and Authorization code.

Itemized Bill - is a detailed bill that contains Service Date, Charges, Procedure, Diagnoses if patient requested, Payment from insurance, Adjustments and Physician name if available.

Definitions:

HCFA 1500 form - This is a standard health insurance claim form used for submitting physician and professional claims to bill the health insurance company.

Itemized Bill - A full list of charges/cost for each item.

W9 form - a W-9 form is a standardized legal document used to collect tax ID and EIN numbers. Completing and submitting Internal Revenue Service (IRS) Form W-9 to third party payers is an important part of timely reimbursement in the revenue cycle of medical providers.

How to request Copy of Receipt, Itemized Receipt, and Itemized Bill?

Copy of Receipt

Create CRM for the copy of receipt and route it to the Itemized Billing requests pool (50032). Make sure to follow the template below so our onshore rep will be aware of what your request is about:
"Patient requests for a copy of receipt to be sent thru (indicate if Mychart or mailing address).
Payment Post Date: MM/DD/YY - MM/DD/YY"

Itemized Receipt

Create CRM and route it to the Itemized Billing requests pool (50032). Make sure to follow the template below so our onshore rep will be aware of what your request is about:
"Patient requests for itemized receipt to be sent thru (indicate if Mychart or mailing address).
Payment Post Date: MM/DD/YY - MM/DD/YY or for the whole year of 20XX"

Itemized Bill

Create CRM and route it to the Itemized Billing requests pool (50032). Make sure to follow the template below so our onshore rep will be aware of what your request is about:
"Patient requests for itemized bill/statement to be sent thru (indicate if Mychart or mailing address).
Statement Date Range: MM/DD/YY - MM/DD/YY or for the whole year of 20XX"

Reminder to all itemized request

Date should be on number format not words. Do not include the receipt number/authorization code/invoice number/unnecessary requests.
