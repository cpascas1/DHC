Refund

Refund

Basic rule for refund: Patient is eligible if there's no self-pay and insurance pending on the account.

Example:

How to request the refund in Epic:

  1. Go to Prof Tx Inquiry and look for undistributed credits.
  2. Select the amount and right click. Under actions, select Refund.
  3. A pop-up will appear. For credit card refund, please select AC214 - REFUND CREDIT CARD [AC214] as the refund code. For check refund, select AC708 - REFUND PATIENT [AC708].
  4. Make sure the amount is correct and method of payment used is accurate.

Reminders:
Again, you can request the refund in Epic directly if patient is eligible and doesn't have any current self-pay balance and insurance pending amounts. If the patient insist for a refund and has a balance or pending amount on the account kindly reach out to your TLs/SMEs to have it reviewed but do not promise or guarantee anything to the patient.
If the patient calls back after 14 days and refund has been initiated by the first inbound agent but still under pending refund status kindly create CRM to Credit Balance pool for the escalation. If the patient calls back again, you can reroute the existing CRM to Credit Balance - Supervisor pool as the second escalation.

  1. Select the appropriate Reason and click Accept.
