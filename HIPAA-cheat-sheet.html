HIPAA Cheat Sheet

PHONE CALL RECEIVED

For HIPAA verification, If the caller's NAME is not listed in FYI but the caller's phone number and relationship is listed on the FYI or on the Consent form that we have on file, then we can release the billing information of the patient to the caller.
