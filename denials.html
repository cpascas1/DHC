Denials

Medical Necessity

Patient called to dispute a medical necessity denial

Patient requests a Letter of Medical Necessity for their own appeal to their health plan

Create a CRM ticket using the pool CRM Optum Coding - Coding Review. (Refer to Duly Create CRM in Epic)
Note: User should make sure first that there is no existing CRM ticket created for the same concern.

Patient should contact the physician's nurse line to request a Letter of Medical Necessity. If the patient is going to appeal with their plan and not pay any open balances-customer service should suggest a payment plan.
