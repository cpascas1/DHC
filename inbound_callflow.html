Call Flow

Inbound Call Flow

The following guidelines should be followed for all inbound calls.

Each call received should be documented on the patients account using activity Optum Inbound FCR [691] or create a CRM if additional follow up is needed.

Step Instructions Suggested Script
Step 1:
Opening		 Deliver the Opening Spiel within 5 seconds “Thank you for calling Duly Health and Care. This is ________________. How may I assist you?”
Step 2:
Acknowledgement		 Acknowledge the caller’s concern “I’ll be happy to _______________________”
“Let me assist you on ______________________”

For example: “I’ll be happy to update your insurance information.”
Step 3:
Authentication

HIPAA Verify the account

Note: Obtain parent’s full name if they call regarding a minor (child) account.

  • For third party caller, check for HIPAA consent form on file. If none, verbal authorization from the patient is required and that is for one time use only.
  • When caller is spouse of patient then provide financial responsibilities only as open balance and payments.
  • When caller is calling from the law office, ask for HIPAA consent to be sent through email to collections@duly.com and fax to 630-469-9686.
  • When caller is insurance representative, check the date of service in question and we can proceed once it is assured that the same insurance is billed for that date of service.
  • When patient wants to add authorized person on the account, HIPAA consent form needs to be submitted through MyChart - Login Page (dupagemd.com) or send the form through email to collections@duly.com and fax to 630-469-9686.
  • For HIPAA consent form, patient can refer to HIPAA Privacy Practices & Forms | Duly Health and Care - DuPage Medical Group.

Associate should not disclose any Personal Health Information to any un-authorized person including third party callers.

 Patient is the Caller:

"For security purposes may I have your name and Date of Birth please?" "May I also have your full address" (Or any of the ff: phone number or last four of member's SSN).

Obtain Caller’s Callback Number:

“May I have your callback number, please?”

Caller is not the patient:

"May I have your full name?"

"May I know your relationship with the patient"
Step 4:
Address Concerns		 Address Caller’s Concerns
Step 5:
Recap/Payment Collection		 Recap actions taken and/or key points discussed during the call and provide outstanding balance and request payment

End of Call Script

“I have taken the following actions on your account (insert actions) when reviewing I can see that you have an outstanding balance of $____________. Are you able to pay in full today or can I help you set up a payment plan?

Bad Debt Balances

"I am not sure if you are aware but you also have a balance of $_____ with the collection agency. If you would like I can accept payment in full today."

Payments Plans must be referred to the collection agency.

NCC - 630-528-5100
Merchants 888-249-3811

Payments

If yes, _______ resume with payment details. “Thank you for your payment, is there anything else I can assist you with today?

Declined – Financial Barrier

If no, patient states they are having difficulty making any payments at all. “I understand that you are experiencing financial barriers at this time, Duly Health and Care does offer a once-per-lifetime opportunity to apply for Financial Hardship where if approved, your balance may be eligible for a reduction from 50-100% of your bill. Is this a program you would like to apply for?”

Screen patient for financial hardship. Provide patient the information needed to apply, and if the patient would like to resume with the application, mark account with FH Pending status using the financial hardship activity note.

Declined Payment Scripting

If no, (review charges to determine if patient is close to collections). “I understand you are unable to make a payment today. Duly Health and Care Policy states that any unpaid balances may be sent to collections at 120 days or after four statements. Please be sure to call us back or set up a formal payment arrangement on MyChart.”

Step 6:
Closing		 Offer additional assistance and offer the survey

Additional Assistance

“Is there anything else I can assist you with today?”
If yes, resume with request.
If no, Offer Survey

Survey Offered

“Would you be willing to take a brief survey regarding the service I provided you today?

If no, “No problem, thank you for calling Duly Health and Care, enjoy the rest of your day.”

If yes, “Thank you for calling Duly Health and Care, please stay on the line while I connect you, enjoy the rest of your day.”
