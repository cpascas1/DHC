Duly Registration Process for Contact Center

If... Then...
Merging of accounts
Note: Refer to Duly Account Merge Job Aid for additional information.
  • Create a CRM ticket to the CRM Registration Request. (Refer to Duly Create CRM in Epic)
  • Provide the CRM ID to the caller. Process complete.
New account or separation of accounts
  • Create a CRM ticket to the P REG CUST SVC REQ. (Refer to Duly Create CRM in Epic)
  • Provide the CRM ID to the caller. Process complete.

Determine if coverage is added or updated on the account.

If... Then...
Yes Proceed to next step.

From the main patient screen click the Tx Inquiry tab.

Determine if there is an existing charge on the account.

If... Then...
Yes Proceed to next step.
No Proceed to Step 20.

Click Edit Visit Coverages link.

Click the "+" icon of the appropriate coverage to add in the section.

Note:

  • Added coverage will automatically be assigned as the secondary payer.
  • Click the up or down arrow to update the filing order.
  • Click the "x" icon if a coverage needs to be removed.

Click Accept.

Click Refresh to view the updated coverage information.

Determine the level of the added coverage/payer.

If... Then...
Primary payer Proceed to Step 20.
Note: After adding/updating the primary payer, below are the possible scenarios that will occur:
  • If charge is clean, it will be posted as a new visit to the new coverage.
  • If charge encounters an error, the visit disappears and will go to the charge review work queue for updating and reposting.
Secondary payer Proceed to next step.

Right-click on the appropriate charge and select Next Responsible Party to transfer the liability to the added coverage.

Select the appropriate payer in the Send To field.

Input the appropriate comment if applicable and then click Accept.

Click the Visit Info folder.

Click New Note.

Input the appropriate details on what was done in the account and then click Save.

From the Registration screen, click Finish once verification checks are complete and visit coverage is updated.
