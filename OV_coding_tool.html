Office Visit Codes

Below are the codes used for the office visits.

Office or Other Outpatient Services
99202-99205 New Patient
99211-99215 Established Patient

Note:

  • We can provide the range to be paid for these codes.
  • Advise the patients that these will only be for office visit codes which do not include the provider's procedures or testing.
