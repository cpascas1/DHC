Credit Balance (P CRM OPTUM CREDIT BALANCE - 100419)

1. Refund Request
Check if the account has $0 Self-pay balance and there is a $0 pending with insurance, then right click on undistributed payment and click on refund.

If the patient calls back again, you can reroute the existing CRM to Credit Balance – Supervisor pool as the second escalation. If the patient calls back again, after CRM was routed to Credit Balance – Supervisor pool as the second escalation with still no update, send CRM to supports for escalation. If the refund has been completed and CRM is still open, close CRM.

2. Transferring Payment to an account with a different service area
Create CRM and properly document payment details and indicate the account number where the payment should be transferred to.

 If the patient calls back after 14 days and the payment transfer has not been transferred, route the CRM to credit balance supervisor. (P CRM OPTUM CREDIT BALANCE - SUPERVISOR REQUEST) If the patient calls back again, and the refund has not been completed, send to supports for follow-up. Close CRM if transfer of payment has been completed
