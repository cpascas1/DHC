New No-Show billing process

Highlights:

  • All Doctors Appointments that have a late Cancellation or a No-Show Fee will be charged a No-Show Fee of $50.00
  • All procedures will have a $150.00 No Show Fee applied
  • Same Day Scheduled Appointments will not have a fee for late cancellation but will be subject to a true No-Show Fee
  • Behavioral Health No Show Fee's will be reduced to $50.00
  • A Once Per Lifetime No Show Fee will be honored. Please utilize AC205 and document the account accordingly
  • If the patient has previously received a courtesy adjustment, no other adjustments will be approved. Limited exceptions for hospitalization, accidents, and/or death of family member will be accepted with management approval.
  • Patients that have Medicaid or are Medicaid eligible are exempt from No Show Fees
