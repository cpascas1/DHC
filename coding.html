Coding

This sheet will help you understand coding concerns.

Code Type Description Resolution
G0402 Welcome to Medicare Visit Patient is eligible on this for the First year that they becomes Medicare Part B member.
G0438 Initial Wellness Visit Patient is eligible on this after the First year that they becomes Medicare Part B member but within 2 years only, this is also once in a lifetime code.
G0439 Subsequent Wellness Visit
A4550 Photo, Surgical Trays, Snare and Scope/Colon Scope Reach out to AM/SME for adjustment
If the balance is already sent to collections, adjustment can no longer be made		 These are all related to surgeries, advise patient that we can adjust the balance and that bill can be disregarded. Do not proactively offer.
87641 MRSA Reach out to AM/SME for adjustment If you encounter this cpt code, this can be adjusted by your support/leaders and put a note that this was approved by RCM.
90750 Zoster Vaccine Regardless of the DOS, we can longer adjust the amount. Patient will need to request HCFA and they need to send it to their pharmacy benefits insurance to be billed. Advise the patient that we don't bill prescription plan and that we can only send HCFA and they are responsible of billing their Medicare part D coverage
A9270 Non-Covered item / Service Reach out to AM/SME to apply discount If there is no adjustments/payments from the insurance, advise the patient that we can give 25% discount for this service.
95199 Self - pay code. Patients are aware that this is not a billable code to insurance and that they have to pay it at the time of service.
75571 Patients who fail to pay the discounted promotional amount of $75 at the time of service no longer qualify for it. The patients will have to pay the full price if they opt to pay at a later date. Self - Pay Write off - Patient Responsibility is $75 only. Remaining is for adjustment. Claim wont be submitted to insurance and will be tagged as "Do not bill".
99211 Office Visit with U0003/U0005
U0005 According to State law of IL, this code is PR. If claim is not billed to insurance, you may attach the active insurance thru Edit Visit Coverage, if the insurance no longer active we can create CRM to Registration for claim submission.
Claim has "Do not bill" to insurance If coding team advised that they do not assign coverage kindly reroute the CRM to the registration. Create CRM route it to Coding for review if the codes are entered correctly and request to bill the insurance once reviewed.
